Research Interests

  • in-memory database systems
  • database system architectures
  • performance issues
  • distributed database systems

    • Hao ZHANG is supervised under Prof. Beng Chin Ooi, and is a member of NUS Database System Research Group. His research interests include in-memory database systems, database system architectures, performance issues, and distributed database systems. He has been working on the in-memory database system - MemepiC since 2012, with focus on the performance issues inherited from the traditional database architecture, new challenges on in-memory database systems, and utilization of newly-emerged hardware (e.g., RDMA, HTM, NUMA, NVRAM).

    He published several papers on top journals and conferences, such as VLDB, ICDE, TKDE, and he is also a reviewer of TKDE and the journal of Big Data Research.
